## Design Context

### Users
Residents, journalists, civic volunteers, RWAs, and local advocates in Uttar Pradesh who see a public issue and need to know where accountability begins and how escalation works.

### Brand Personality
Clear, civic, firm. The product should feel like a public-interest tool: calm enough for stressful moments, precise enough to be trusted, and direct enough to help a person act.

### Aesthetic Direction
Operational civic dashboard with a bright paper-like surface, strong typography, restrained official colors, and dense but legible information. It should avoid a marketing landing page, decorative spectacle, or dark "tech" styling.

### Design Principles
- Start with the citizen's problem and location, then reveal responsibility.
- Separate verified officials from role-level placeholders with visible confidence.
- Make escalation legible from local office to Chief Minister.
- Turn results into action through a ready-to-send email draft.
- Keep the interface fast, readable, and useful on mobile.
