# Contacts

Contacts are **official, public channels only** — office emails, helplines, grievance portals (e.g. CPGRAMS), official websites, and published social handles.

## What we collect

- Office email addresses (`contact_type = email`)
- Published phone/helpline numbers
- Grievance portal URLs
- Official website and social media links

## What we do NOT collect

- Personal mobile numbers
- Private email addresses
- Unpublished contact details

Contacts can attach to a **position**, **person**, **body**, or **jurisdiction**. Position-level contacts are preferred when available.
